Premise Entry Monitoring System (PEMS)



In a world where security is volatile, the quest for comprehensive security has never been higher. Manual means of verifying visitors’ identities is not only expensive but also frosted with problems making it inadequate for actionable security intelligence. In many facilities, visitors are still registered using a handwritten paper log. While this method is perceived as quick and easy, it provides virtually no security and leaves visitor information available for anyone to see. Automated visitor management solutions lend a more professional appearance, enhance security and meet compliance mandates for the collection and auditing of visitor data. Visitor registration & management is a paramount concern for every intelligent building or facility. Having the ability to screen and track them can contribute substantially to the safety and protection of your organization’s employees and property. Visitor Management system provides a way to effectively control, record & track your organization's visitor traffic. It helps to prevent the entry of any unauthorized or unwanted person in the premises. The flexible architecture of Visitor Tracker allows various people in the organization to directly access the data they need. Integrated with various hardware like webcam, RFID Readers, bar-code scanner & printers. Email/SMS/popup alerts for visitor notification. Available in client-server & Enterprise versions.

Premise Entry Monitoring Solutions (PEMS™) is an innovative real-time identity and movement tracking solution for individuals entering into sensitive premises designed for both corporate and non-corporate entities.

The system ensures that thousands of employees, customers, vendors and visitors passing through your corporate lobby are approved and safe to be in your building. PEMS™ screens visitors to sensitive offices or private locations and prevent unauthorized entries while providing comprehensive audit trails of all visitors’ details – ID, photos, cars and other assets – entering the location.

Installing PEMS™ entry management in your organization send to all visitors that safety is a top priority to you and thereby improving your corporate image. PEMS™ is a simple yet power full solution that streamlines visitors’ sign-in process, tracks everyone who enters your building and stops any threat at the door.

The system allows all visitors to use their PCs, phones and tablets (via online portal and mobile apps) to create an appointment from any location and select a Host Employee or Office to visit in advance. Visitation request must be approved before any visitor could gain entry into the premise keeping your employees more focused and productive than ever before.
